THEORETICAL FOUNDATION
======================

Based on Gal & Ghahramani (ICML 2016):
"Dropout as a Bayesian Approximation: Representing Model Uncertainty
in Deep Learning"

Implementation captures epistemic uncertainty (model uncertainty) through
variance of T stochastic forward passes with dropout enabled.
